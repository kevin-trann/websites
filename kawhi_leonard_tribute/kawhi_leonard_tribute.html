Kawhi Leonard

"The Claw"

    Timeline of Kawhi Leonard's Career

  • 2011 – Drafted by the Indiana Pacers (15th overall), then traded to the San Antonio Spurs on draft night
  • 2014 – Wins NBA Championship and Finals MVP with the Spurs.
  • 2016 – Named NBA Defensive Player of the Year (second time; first was in 2015).
  • 2017 – Suffers ankle injury in Western Conference Finals vs. Warriors.
  • 2018 – Traded to the Toronto Raptors.
  • 2019 – Wins NBA Championship and Finals MVP with the Raptors.
  • 2019 – Signs with the Los Angeles Clippers.
  • 2020 – Helps Clippers reach Western Conference Semifinals in the NBA bubble
  • 2021 – Advances to Western Conference Finals for the first time in Clippers franchise history.
  • 2024 – Continues as a core player for the Clippers and a veteran leader in the league.
